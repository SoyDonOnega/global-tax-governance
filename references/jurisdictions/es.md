- **Supranational:** Treaty of EU + Directives (DAC6/7/8 · 2022/2523 · 2021/2101 · AI Act) · OECD Model + TPG + Pillar Two Rules · MCAA · CRS · FATCA · Spanish DTT network

## 8 — REVIEWER NOTES

- Spanish tax professional secrecy (Art. 45 LGT) covers **abogados only**, not Big Four advisors who are not lawyers. Verify the intermediary's exact qualification before invoking.
- TEAC unifications of criterion under Art. 229 LGT bind the administration retroactively — surface the legitimate-expectations argument when applicable.
- Operaciones vinculadas (Art. 18 LIS) — the documentation burden and the comparability standard align with OECD TPG but the formal documentation requirements have local specifics; verify against the latest RGGI revision.
- Intercompany service charges — deductibility at the recipient end is challenged under Art. 15.e LIS (gastos no deducibles) when the benefit / WTP test is not evidenced. AEAT inspectors look for: written request from the recipient, acknowledgement of delivery, cost-base traceability to documented work, exclusion of shareholder/duplicative/incidental activity. The `tp-analysis benefit-test` subcommand is the canonical workflow.
- TS Sala III doctrine consolidates with two judgments (criterio reiterado); a single judgment is strong criterion but not binding.
- The CBPT is voluntary but adherence affects the practical relationship with AEAT — declared adherents get faster, less adversarial inspection in practice.
