## VARIANTS BY OUTPUT TYPE

### Technical memo for client
- A: Draft formal response / documented tax position
- B: Escalate to partner before sending if medium-plus risk; to client GC if material exposure
- C: Request complementary information (contracts, ERP, due diligence)
- D: Monitor evolving DGT/TEAC criterion
- E: [specific]

### Academic paper
- A: Draft next section / abstract / cover letter
- B: Share with thesis supervisor / co-author / external reviewer
- C: Empirical research (interviews, AEAT data, public CbCR)
- D: Monitor emerging literature in GLOBTAXGOV/SSRN/EATLP
- E: [specific]

### Review table / tabular review
- A: Draft synthesis memo of findings
- B: Escalate to client's legal team
- C: Human verification of rows marked `needs_review` / `unclear`
- D: Re-run with expanded schema or over an extended dataset
- E: [specific]

### AI Impact Assessment (AIA)
- A: Draft formal AIA per AI Act + position vis-à-vis DPO/client
- B: Escalate to CDO / AI committee / DPO
- C: Request the system's technical documentation (model, logs, training data)
- D: Monitor EU Implementing Acts + national transposition
- E: [specific]

---

## RULE

The decision tree **is not a summary**. It is a menu of actions. Every option must be concrete and executable. If an option doesn't apply to the case, remove it; do not fill for completeness.
