# GREY-AREAS CATALOGUE BY TAX

For the `query-email` skill to produce determinative questions and not noise. Each entry: trigger condition · question template · normative basis.

> Note: Spanish legal article references (Art. X LIS, LIVA, LIRPF, RIS, etc.) and form codes (modelo 200, 232, 303, etc.) are preserved as proper-noun citations. Substantive legal concepts in Spanish (BI, BIN, ISP, BDR, etc.) are likewise preserved.

## CORPORATE INCOME TAX (IS) — Modelo 200

### Bad-debt provisions (BDR / créditos comerciales / insolvencias)
- **Trigger:** any allowance or balance of receivables / insolvencies provision in the support.
- **Question template:** Is the receivable ≥ 6 months past due? Is the debtor in concurso (insolvency)? Is there judicial reclamation? Is the debtor a public entity (non-deductible unless in concurso)?
- **Basis:** Art. 13.1.e LIS · DGT V0214-18 and subsequent rulings.

### Generic (non-specific) provisions
- **Trigger:** allowance to provision without identification of a specific debtor.
- **Question:** Is it a global (non-specific) provision? If so, non-deductible — must be reversed in BI.
- **Basis:** Art. 13.1 LIS contrario sensu.

### Client entertainment expenses (atenciones a clientes)
- **Trigger:** account 627 / 629 / line items marked as entertainment / public relations / gifts.
- **Question:** Does it meet the promotional character test (vs. gratuity)? Amount relative to 1% of turnover.
- **Basis:** Art. 15.e LIS.

### Donations
- **Trigger:** account 678 / contributions to foundations / NGOs.
- **Question:** Is the recipient covered by Law 49/2002? Donation certificate received? Does Art. 20 Law 49/2002 deduction apply (35% / 40%)?
- **Basis:** Art. 15.e LIS · Law 49/2002.

### Director compensation
- **Trigger:** payment to board member / director.
- **Question:** Do the bylaws provide for paid status? Approved by general meeting? Executive function with separate contract?
- **Basis:** Art. 15.e LIS · TS case-law on "doctrina del vínculo" (now superseded) · STS of [verify latest update].

### Sanctions, surcharges, fines
- **Trigger:** account 678 / traffic fines / administrative sanctions / late-payment interest with AEAT.
- **Question:** Is it an administrative sanction or late-payment interest in favour of the Administration? Non-deductible.
- **Basis:** Art. 15.c LIS.

### Transactions with listed (non-cooperative) jurisdictions
- **Trigger:** counterparty in a listed jurisdiction (EU non-cooperative list or Spanish BOE list).
- **Question:** Justification of effective operation + valid economic reasons. Reinforced documentation.
- **Basis:** Art. 15.g LIS · DGT binding rulings · updated AEAT list.

### Impairment of equity instruments (Art. 21 LIS)
- **Trigger:** impairment allowance on participations.
- **Question:** Does the participation meet the Art. 21 exemption requirements? If yes, impairment is non-deductible.
- **Basis:** Art. 13.2.b LIS · Art. 21 LIS.

### Net operating loss carry-forwards (BINs)
- **Trigger:** compensation of BINs.
- **Question:** Documented with accounts + filed tax returns? Within the Art. 26 LIS limit? Year of generation not prescribed for audit purposes (Art. 26.5 LIS — 10 years)?
- **Basis:** Art. 26 LIS.

### R&D&i credit (deducción I+D+i)
- **Trigger:** application of Chapter IV LIS credit.
- **Question:** Binding Reasoned Report (IMV) from MICINN obtained? DGT consulta? Technical project support?
- **Basis:** Arts. 35-39 LIS · RD 1432/2003.

### Intangible licensing — patent box (Art. 23 LIS)
- **Trigger:** revenue from licensing / use of intangibles.
- **Question:** Does the Art. 23 reduction apply? Nexus ratio computed? Specific documentation under Art. 23.6?
- **Basis:** Art. 23 LIS · RIS.

### DTA / DTL — temporary differences
- **Trigger:** deferred tax assets / liabilities on the balance sheet.
- **Question:** Documented reversal plan? Sufficient probability of future BI generation? Monetisable DTAs vs. ordinary (Art. 130 LIS)?
- **Basis:** NRV 13ª PGC · Art. 130 LIS.

### Reorganisations
- **Trigger:** merger, split, exchange, contribution.
- **Question:** Does the special regime of Chapter VII Title VII LIS apply? Valid economic motive (Art. 89.2)? AEAT communication filed?
- **Basis:** Chapter VII Title VII LIS · Art. 89 LIS.

### Capital subsidies
- **Trigger:** account 130 / 131 / 132.
- **Question:** Recognition in profit per NRV 18ª? Tax treatment aligned.
- **Basis:** Art. 17 LIS · NRV 18ª PGC.

## VAT (IVA) — Modelos 303 / 390

### Intra-Community transactions
- **Trigger:** invoices from EU suppliers or sales to EU customers.
- **Question:** EU VAT-ID validated in VIES at the transaction date? Transport documentation (intra-EU delivery)?
- **Basis:** Art. 25 LIVA · EU Regulation 282/2011.

### Reverse charge (ISP — inversión sujeto pasivo)
- **Trigger:** invoices without VAT charged from EU / third-country supplier / services located in Spain (TAI).
- **Question:** Correct application of Art. 84? No double charge + self-declaration.
- **Basis:** Art. 84 LIVA.

### Prorrata
- **Trigger:** taxpayer with exempt and non-exempt transactions.
- **Question:** General prorrata (provisional / definitive) vs. special prorrata? Option exercised on time.
- **Basis:** Arts. 102-106 LIVA.

### Exempt transactions
- **Trigger:** sales without VAT.
- **Question:** Art. 20 LIVA exemption (without right of deduction) or Art. 25 LIVA (with right of deduction)? Justification.
- **Basis:** Arts. 20 / 25 LIVA.

### Capital goods — regularisation
- **Trigger:** acquisition of fixed assets or change in affected use.
- **Question:** Regularisation of input VAT due during the regularisation period (5/10 years)?
- **Basis:** Art. 107 LIVA.

### Self-supply (autoconsumo)
- **Trigger:** free delivery or change of allocation.
- **Question:** Taxable event under Art. 9 or Art. 12 LIVA? Tax base?
- **Basis:** Arts. 9, 12, 79 LIVA.

### BI modification for non-payment (Art. 80.4)
- **Trigger:** uncollectable receivables with VAT charged.
- **Question:** 1-year / 6-month period met? Judicial claim or notarial requirement? AEAT communication on time? Total modification period not exceeded?
- **Basis:** Art. 80.4 LIVA · Art. 24 RIVA.

### Cash-basis regime (RECC)
- **Trigger:** taxpayer adhered to RECC.
- **Question:** Transaction volume kept < 2M€? Properly communicated to clients? Accrual and deduction computed on receipt/payment basis?
- **Basis:** Arts. 163 decies-sexies LIVA.

## WITHHOLDING — IRPF / IRNR

### Payments to residents of listed jurisdictions
- **Trigger:** payer / recipient in a listed jurisdiction.
- **Question:** Applicable rate (no CDI: 24%/19%/other depending on nature). Residence documentation.
- **Basis:** Art. 25 LIRNR · AEAT listed-jurisdiction register.

### CDI application
- **Trigger:** reduced withholding invoking a tax treaty.
- **Question:** Recipient's current tax residence certificate? LOB / PPT clause met if the CDI has one? AEAT form filed?
- **Basis:** Applicable CDI model · Order HAC/3626/2003 and amendments.

### Art. 7 LIRPF exempt income
- **Trigger:** payment labelled exempt (severance, per diems, expatriates).
- **Question:** Meets the conditions of the relevant subsection (e.g., Art. 7.p — cap 60,100€ + non-listed country + analogous tax)? Supporting documentation?
- **Basis:** Art. 7 LIRPF.

### Benefits in kind
- **Trigger:** company car, health insurance, pension plan.
- **Question:** Correct valuation (Art. 43 LIRPF). On-account payment computed and applied.
- **Basis:** Arts. 42-43 LIRPF.

## MODELO 232 — RELATED-PARTY TRANSACTIONS

### Reportable transactions
- **Trigger:** related-party transactions > 250k€ (same counterparty) or > 100k€ "specific".
- **Question:** Correct key codification? Local-file documentation current?
- **Basis:** Art. 18 LIS · Order HFP/816/2017 · Modelo 232.

### Transactions with listed jurisdictions
- **Trigger:** related counterparty in a listed jurisdiction.
- **Question:** Mandatory reporting regardless of threshold.
- **Basis:** Art. 18 LIS · Order HFP/816/2017.

### Patent box (Art. 23 LIS)
- **Trigger:** intra-group intangible licensing.
- **Question:** Reduction applied and reported? Nexus computed.
- **Basis:** Art. 23 LIS.

## PILLAR TWO / GLOBE

### Covered taxes — inclusion
- **Trigger:** taxes paid or accrued in the fiscal year.
- **Question:** Inclusion per Pillar Two Model Rules Art. 4? WHT included? Other local taxes?
- **Basis:** OECD Pillar Two Model Rules Art. 4 · Directive (EU) 2022/2523.

### GloBE Income — adjustments
- **Trigger:** P&L line items requiring a GloBE adjustment.
- **Question:** Dividend exclusion applied? Equity gain/loss exclusion? Stock-based compensation timing? IRC §163(j) / equivalents?
- **Basis:** Pillar Two Model Rules Art. 3.

### Transitional CbCR safe harbour
- **Trigger:** intention to apply the safe harbour.
- **Question:** Passes Routine Profits Test? De Minimis (revenue < 10M€ + profit < 1M€)? Simplified ETR Test (> 15% 2024 / > 16% 2025 / > 17% 2026)?
- **Basis:** OECD Safe Harbour and Penalty Relief (December 2022) + Administrative Guidance.

### QDMTT
- **Trigger:** jurisdiction with implemented QDMTT.
- **Question:** Correctly applied? Reduces IIR/UTPR top-up to zero?
- **Basis:** Pillar Two Model Rules + local transposition.

## ACCOUNTING — TAX RECONCILIATION (generic)

### Misclassified temporary differences
- **Trigger:** temporary adjustment without documented reversal.
- **Question:** Expected reversal year? Consistent with business plan?

### Period attribution
- **Trigger:** invoices near closing.
- **Question:** Correct accrual? Closing provisions?

### Exchange rates
- **Trigger:** transactions in foreign currency.
- **Question:** Rate applied (ECB / closing / monthly average)? Source?

## EXAMPLE OF A WELL-DRAFTED QUESTION (the standard to follow)

❌ **Bad:** "Is the receivables provision deductible?"

✅ **Good:**
> **Insolvency provision — Client X · balance 145,230 €**
>
> For deductibility under Art. 13.1.e LIS we need to confirm **one** of the following (not all required, just one):
> (i) Is the receivable ≥ 6 months past due at year-end?
> (ii) Is the debtor declared in concurso (insolvency)?
> (iii) Has the debtor been prosecuted for alzamiento de bienes (asset concealment)?
> (iv) Is there a judicial claim or arbitral proceeding under way for collection?
>
> If none of the above, we must treat the expense as **non-deductible** and reflect it as a positive permanent adjustment to BI. Estimated tax impact: ~36,300 € additional liability at 25%.
